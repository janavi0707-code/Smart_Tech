Scrum Framework

Roles

  • Product Owner: Manages the product backlog.
  • Scrum Master: Facilitates the process.
  • Development Team: Builds the product.

Artifacts

  • Product Backlog: A prioritized list of features.
  • Sprint Backlog: Subset of the product backlog for the current sprint.
  • Increment: The sum of all completed product backlog items.

Events

  • Sprint: A time-boxed period for completing work.
  • Daily Scrum: A 15-minute daily meeting for team updates.
  • Sprint Review: At the end of the sprint, demonstrate what was built.
  • Sprint Retrospective: A meeting to reflect and improve processes.
